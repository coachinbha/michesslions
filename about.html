Our Growth Story

From Community Club to Championship Winners

What started as a small community chess club in 2017 has evolved into a local chess education organization. Through dedication, expert instruction, and a commitment to excellence, Chess Lions has helped hundreds of students discover their potential and achieve championship success.

2017

The Beginning

Chess Lions was founded as a small community chess club at the Novi Public Library with a handful of enthusiastic students. Our mission was simple: make chess accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

2018

Expanding Programs

Introduced structured beginner and intermediate programs. Student enrollment doubled as word spread about our effective teaching methods and welcoming environment.

2019

First Tournament Success

We ran our first ever chess tournament at the Novi Library drawing over 50 players and gained city-wide recognition.

2020

Digital Transformation

Adapted to online learning during challenging times. Launched virtual classes and online tournaments, expanding our reach beyond geographic boundaries.

2023

Wins at state events

The Chess Lions team, at the time called "Windsor Chess Lions", would go on to win the K-8 Michigan Club Championship and set a record of 18.0/20 total points with 3 flawless finishers, a record we would come back to break in 2025.

2024-2025

Event Expansion

Continued producing state championship winning teams to finish a hat-rick of 1st place finishes. Started to run more tournaments and events that steadily grew to reaching over 100 players. Secured local sponsorships and deepened our library partnerships across South Lyon and Metro Detroit.
