About Us

Experienced Chess Instructors

We are the Chess Lions, dedicated to promoting the ancient game of strategy and intellect across North America. Starting out as a community chess club in 2016, we've grown into a championship-producing organization. We've proudly coached, taught, and led teams to victory in several state-level championships in both Canada and the USA. With our proven methods and passionate instruction, everyone can be a champion.
